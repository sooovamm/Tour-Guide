Tawang – Enjoy the Surreal

From the boundaries of the sky the dancing clouds smile at the snow-blanketed passes. The entire Tawang region is nestled amidst Gudpi and Chong Chugmi ranges and bathed by the water of Tawang Chu River. Here mother nature is busy in healing people with its soothing vibes, the snowcapped mountains from the east, the dense jungles on the way seducing the eyes and senses, pristine and surreal Buddhist monasteries are other charming attractions for some peaceful moments of self-introspection. The views of lakes and gorgeous waterfalls will fill you with joy and elevated thoughts. Indeed, Tawang is one of the best tourist places to visit in Arunachal Pradesh. The best time to visit Tawang is the month of May. 2-3 days are enough to explore the land.

Best Places to Visit & Things to Do in Tawang Arunachal:

  • If you want to have a look at the historical sites set on an emerald green meadow, Tawang is the ideal place for you. Tawang Monastery is 113 feet long and 80 feet wide edifice which is a famous Buddhist temple. The interior of the building is stunningly beautiful and a 28 feet high golden statue of Lord Buddha is worshiped here.
  • The Indo-China border is another significant tourist spot in Tawang. Jaswant Garh Tawang War Memorial, Tawang Craft Center, Brahma-dung-chung Ani Gompa, Urgelling Gompa are some of the important sightseeing places.
