Dona Paula

Situated on the suburbs of Panaji, Dona Paula is one of the popular tourist attractions in Goa. It is recognized as the home of the National Institute of Oceanography. In fact, it is a hub for numerous accommodation options in Goa. It is a hammered shaped land that divides the estuaries of Zuari and Mandovi River. The Dona Paula monument is located on a small islet, which is connected with the mainland by a small bridge and a quay. Dona Paula has a beautiful stretch of sea that remains crowded; however, during the monsoon the stretch is calm and becomes a lovely haunt for romance.
