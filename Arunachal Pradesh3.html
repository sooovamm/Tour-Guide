Bomdila – Definition of Enchantment

Bomdila – Definition of Enchantment

The headquarters of West Kameng District Bomdila has been captivating the eyes of tourists for ages. You will definitely get a multicultural experience in one place. The lifestyle of Aka, Miji, Monpa, Khawas and Sherdukpen tribes inhabits this wonderful place. Kangte and Gorichen Peaks will seduce your soul the moment you behold them. The monasteries here will take you to the world of serenity and spiritual wisdom. Blessed with stunning views, apple orchards, scenic roads, unique ethnographic museum and craft center, this place is unique, surreal and a real source of enchantment. Bomdila is also an attractive summer vacation getaway in Arunachal Pradesh. This wonderful getaway is located at a height of 8000 ft above sea level. Idyllic destination for family outing, Bomdila will indulge you to get closer with your loved ones.

Best Places to Visit & Things to Do in Bomdila:

  • Stroll across the land to have a glimpse of the snow packed Himalayan peaks and clearer views of Kangto and Gorichen Peak. If you are adventurous enough you can trek through the hills of different levels or simply stroll through the long quiet trails of the luxurious slopes.
  • The most important sightseeing spot in Bomdila is Bomdila Monastery which is rich in art and craft. The Local Craft Center displays samples of local craft of Bomdila.
  • If you are ready to take a short tour, visit Sessa Orchid Sanctuary where 2600 orchids of 80 species are cultivated. To witness the orchid cultivation more closely, trekking routes are developed which is truly amazing.
